Lead Time Math for Small Distributors

Quick math · Updated 2025-08-01

To avoid lost orders, buffer against manufacturing time + shipping + customs. Keep a minimum safety stock that covers your average weekly sales × the longest step in your chain.

  • Manufacturing: 10–20 days
  • Shipping: 3–7 days air, 20–40 days sea
  • Customs: 2–7 days

Start small, measure, then tune safety stock by category.
